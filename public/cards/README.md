# PatientTrac Homepage Card Images — Parent Brand Aligned

This folder contains the seven homepage card images used by `index.html`.

These images replace the temporary stock-photo / stick-figure workflow graphics with premium, application-specific PatientTrac visual artwork aligned to the parent brand board in `assets/PatientTrac_EMR_Final.jpeg`.

## Required filenames

- `forge.jpg` — PatientTrac Forge: patient management, scheduling, billing, claim validation, reimbursement analytics.
- `revela.jpg` — PatientTrac Revela: cosmetic surgery and aesthetic care, facial treatment planning, before/after and outcomes panels.
- `mind.jpg` — PatientTrac Mind: neurobehavioral health EMR, psychiatric documentation, brain/neuro visual, safety, symptom and scale trends.
- `asc.jpg` — PatientTrac ASC: ambulatory surgery center operations, OR room status, case schedule, inventory, supply alerts, DEA controls.
- `continuum.jpg` — PatientTrac Continuum: perioperative/procedural documentation, smart consent, anesthesia monitoring, operative timeline, MAR/implants.
- `profiler.jpg` — PatientTrac Profiler: intelligent patient intake, profile completion, AI-guided intake, secure multilingual intake.
- `companion.jpg` — PatientTrac Companion: recovery, monitoring, engagement, pain/symptom tracking, medication adherence, secure messaging and care-team alerts.

## Parent brand reference

Use the parent PatientTrac brand board as the visual source of truth:

- Deep Blue: `#0B2D4D`
- Teal: `#0097A7`
- Sky Blue: `#4DB6E5`
- Soft Gray: `#E6EBF1`
- Gold: `#D4AF37`
- Typography direction: `Poppins Semibold / Poppins Regular`
- Visual language: clinical network lines, connected nodes, secure/trusted iconography, white/soft-gray surfaces for parent-level brand moments, and dark navy/cyan/gold high-tech treatment for app-specific product visuals.

## Design rules

- Do not use generic stock photos as app cards.
- Do not use stick people or placeholder fallback icons as the primary card visual.
- Do not use a smartwatch or generic wearable image for Companion.
- Keep the card art application-specific and clinically relevant.
- Keep live HTML responsible for product descriptions, CTAs, and accessibility text.
- Maintain strong gold visibility in badges, CTAs, dividers, and brand accents.
