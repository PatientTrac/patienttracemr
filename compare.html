01 — Mind

Three disciplines, one record

Mind spans psychiatry, psychology, and neurology in a single EMR -- not just behavioral health. Evaluations, medication management, and neurological documentation share the same encounter.

02 — Continuum

Multi-specialty procedural episode

Continuum is not limited to one surgery type. It documents the full procedural episode -- pre-op through recovery -- across every specialty on the network.

03 — Companion

RTM, not RPM

Companion uses Remote Therapeutic Monitoring (RTM, CPT 98975-98981) -- purpose-built for recovery and adherence tracking, not general remote physiologic monitoring.

04 — The Spine

One record, one encounter key, RLS, trilingual

Every application reads and writes the same patient and encounter records. Row-level security isolates tenants, and the entire experience runs in English, Spanish, and French.
