Core Workflows

From registration to encounter routing.

01

Patient management

Master patient record (55 fields) with US + 16-country international support: per-country phone, postal, province, and tax-ID formats (SSN, ITIN, EIN, NIT, RFC, CPF, RIF), passport capture, smart address with geo-intelligence (ZIP/postal lookup auto-fills city + state/province).

02

Scheduling & check-in

Multi-provider, multi-facility drag-and-drop calendar; 23 configurable appointment types each mapped to CPT, default fee, POS, duration, and color. Four-step check-in: arrival, insurance verification, encounter open, route to specialty app.

03

The encounter router

cr.open_encounter() mints an encounter_id; checkin_and_route() resolves the provider's specialty against enabled modules and launches the correct app (Revela / Mind / ASC / Continuum) with a cross-app token carrying encounter_id, patient_id, provider_id — no second login, no re-keyed chart.

04

Electronic claims (837P)

True X12 837P generation (005010X222A1) — builds the complete professional claim and transmits to the clearinghouse over OAuth2 (Optum / Change Healthcare). Pluggable international gateways: US 837P live, Colombia RIPS/DIAN and UK NHS registered.

05

ERA/835 & revenue cycle

Imports remittance, parses BPR/TRN/CLP/CAS, auto-matches to superbills, posts payments and adjustments. Nine-tab workspace: superbills, AI coding, EDI, ERA, rejections, A/R aging (0-30/31-60/61-90/91-120/120+), pre-auth, AI-drafted appeals, secondary claims/COB.

06

Proposals & financing

AI-assisted, itemized cost estimates by procedure with financing options (down payment, monthly, term, discount), AI patient-summary narrative, branded PDF output, and email/secure-link delivery. Built for elective/cash-pay conversion.

Clinical Intelligence

AI woven into scheduling and revenue.

AI

No-show prediction

1-10 risk score at booking from prior no-show rate, self-pay status, day/time, visit type, insurance, recency, new-vs-established; high-risk triggers extra reminders, critical suggests double-book.

AI

Smart scheduling

Analyzes a 14-day window and ranks 3 optimal slots with reasons — checking conflicts, no-show risk, insurance visit-interval rules, and specialty patterns.

AI

AI pre-visit intake

Tokenized 48-hour patient link collects chief complaint, meds, symptoms, severity; returns clinician summary, suggested ICD-10, and red-flag triage (emergent/urgent/routine) that pre-fills the encounter.

AI

Wait-time intelligence

Arrived/called-back/seen/departed timestamps; dashboards by provider, day, hour; automatic service-recovery message when wait exceeds 20 minutes.
