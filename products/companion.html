About Companion

Not another siloed monitoring tool.

Most remote-monitoring tools are siloed point solutions. Companion is a trilingual, multi-currency, guardrailed-AI recovery app wired to the same clinical record, the same care team, and the same network key as the EMR the patient was treated in — with the monitoring dashboard built into the practice's existing hub.

Core Workflows

What the patient does. What the care team sees.

01

Patient daily experience

Today screen with daily summary (meds taken, check-in status, latest vital, meals/activity), medication adherence logging, diet journal with AI nutrition Q&A, and exercise/rehab log with AI education.

02

Vitals and device integration

Manual vital entry plus consumer-device OAuth (Fitbit / Withings) for automated physiologic data. Pair, connect, sync, refresh — with audit trail.

03

Journal and red-flag alerts

Daily mood + pain (0-10) + free-text check-in. Red-flag alerts routed to the care team when thresholds are breached. Symptom pattern detection surfaces trends before complications.

04

RTM billing (98975-98981)

Remote therapeutic monitoring revenue capture: device setup, education, data collection, and clinical review time documentation. Patient-reported outcomes and adherence, not FDA-device RPM.

05

Patient health record

Care plan, active meds, lab results (via @patienttrac/clinical-viewer), device readings, education history. Messages: async patient-care-team communication.

06

Billing and accounting

Invoices, payments, insurance reimbursements, coverage card (co-pay/deductible/co-insurance/OOP), multi-currency (USD + COP), AI document extraction with patient review gate (edit/soft-void with required reason).

Features

What the patient sees. What the care team gets.

11 patient-facing screens

Today, Medications, Diet, Exercise, Vitals, Journal, Treatment and Learning, Messages, Progress, Health Record, Billing.

AI with hard clinical guardrails

Non-diagnostic, non-prescriptive. Never diagnoses, never changes medications or doses. General education only, grounded in the patient's own care plan. Emergencies routed to local emergency services.

30-day adherence trending

Medication, pain (0-10), and mood trends visualized for both patient and care team, with threshold-based alerts.

Consumer device OAuth

Fitbit and Withings integration via pair/connect/sync/refresh with native mobile session and audit API.

Recovery episode packages

30, 60, and 90-day bundled monitoring for surgical and procedural follow-up ($29/$49/$79 per patient).

Care-team dashboard in Forge

Monitoring dashboard embedded in Forge (Admin, Companion) — clinicians watch from the console they already use.

Multi-currency billing

USD + COP with invoices, payments, coverage card, and AI document extraction with patient review gate.

RPM-ready software layer

Optional integration layer for practices adding FDA-cleared peripheral device data alongside patient-reported monitoring.

The Connected Journey

How it intertwines.

When the OR case in ASC closes and Continuum finishes the operative record, the patient moves into Companion on the same encounter_id — guided recovery, photo/journal check-ins, adherence, and red-flag alerts that surface in the surgeon's Forge console. The loop that started with Profiler closes here.
