About Revela

The connected, mobile, real-time journey.

Commodity aesthetic software makes a surgeon type into boxes at a desktop after the fact. Revela lets them photograph, draw on the patient's anatomy, run an AI safety check, and hand over a financed proposal — chairside, on an iPad, in real time — on a record that Profiler already populated and that ASC, Continuum, and Companion will carry through surgery and recovery. The differentiator is the connected, mobile, real-time journey, not any single screen.

Core Workflows

From intake to recovery — one record.

01

Before the visit — Profiler

Profiler captures comprehensive patient and family history a plastic-surgery consult depends on — surgical/healing history, pregnancy/breastfeeding, family history of breast cancer — through adaptive AI-branching intake. It lands in the shared record before the patient sits down.

02

In the room — iPhone/iPad

Chairside: capture clinical photos in real time, draw directly on professional anatomical SVG templates, document in region-specific modules (Face, Breast, Body, Liposuction), run an AI surgical-safety screen, and generate a patient proposal — all while the patient is still in the chair.

03

AI surgical safety

Contraindications, drug interactions, missing pre-op, risk factors — built on validated scores: Caprini, STOP-Bang, RCRI, ASA. Severity-graded safety flags for clinician review.

04

Pricing the case

ProposalBuilder combines the professional/surgeon fee and financing (branded PDF, down-payment/monthly/term options, emailed or secure link) with ASC facility and case costs from preference cards. One patient-facing proposal, professional and itemized.

05

Surgery day

ASC drives the live OR: real-time case console, preference-card supplies and implants, equipment usage, DEA logging. Continuum captures the operative record. Both open on the same encounter_id Revela created.

06

After — Companion

Post-op: guided recovery episodes, photo-based healing check-ins, medication/wound-care adherence, and care-team alerts when something looks off — closing the loop back to the surgeon.

The Connected Journey

How it intertwines.

The connected journey is the story: Profiler front-loads the aesthetic/surgical/family history. Revela documents the consult and generates the proposal chairside. ASC preference cards produce the facility cost, combined into one patient-facing estimate. On surgery day, ASC runs the live OR and Continuum captures the operative record — both on the same encounter_id. Post-op, Companion carries recovery with photo check-ins, adherence tracking, and red-flag alerts back to the surgeon's Forge console.
