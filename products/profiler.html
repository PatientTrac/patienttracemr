About Profiler

Not a static PDF form.

Most "patient portals" are static PDF forms. Profiler is a specialty-adaptive, AI-conversational, trilingual intake engine with red-flag triage that writes structured clinical data directly into the EMR. It captures the comprehensive history a specialty consult depends on — surgical, healing, family, aesthetic — through adaptive branching, so the provider opens a chart that's already complete.

Core Workflows

From token link to completed chart.

01

Token link access

A private ?token= link (48-hour expiry) validated against cr.patient_intake, prefilled with whatever the practice already knows. New patients start at the portal directly.

02

8-step core intake

Personal, Contact & Address, Identity & Demographics, Emergency Contact, Medical History, Review of Systems, Insurance, Consent & Signature. Progress auto-saves and resumes.

03

9 specialty branches

After a "Type of Visit" choice, the patient routes into deep intake: Plastic/Lifestyle, Bariatric, Dermatology, Oncology, Ophthalmology, Orthopedic, Mental-Health screening, Surgical, Systems. General visits skip it.

04

AI conversational intake

Optional natural-language chat (server-side Claude) gathers chief complaint and history, produces structured summary and red-flag triage — detectRedFlags + extractStructuredData.

05

Specialty-tuned prompts

Plastic surgery captures surgical/healing history (keloids, slow healing, excessive bleeding), pregnancy/breastfeeding, family history of breast cancer. Mental health is trauma-informed, warm, non-judgmental.

06

Red-flag triage

Severity-graded concerns surfaced to the provider before the visit: emergent, urgent, and routine classification with specific clinical flags.

Benefits

The Connected Journey

How it intertwines.

Profiler is the history engine for the whole network. A plastic-surgery patient arrives at Revela with their aesthetic, surgical, healing, and family history already in the chart. A behavioral-health patient arrives at Mind pre-screened. No clipboard, no re-keying, no blank chart — the work is done before the patient sits down, on the same cr.patient_intake record every app reads.
